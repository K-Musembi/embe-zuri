About embe zuri

embe zuri web app uses AI deep learning technology to diagnose common mango diseases. Users upload an image of a mango leaf and the app processes the image to determine if it's healthy or diseased. The app offers agronomic advice on the best solutions to manage a mango disease that has been detected.
